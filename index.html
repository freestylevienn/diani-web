Explore Diani Beach

Discover the beauty and activities of Kenya's premier beach destination

Diani Beach

Voted one of Africa's best beaches with powdery white sand and turquoise waters.

Water Sports

Kitesurfing, snorkeling, scuba diving, and more in the warm Indian Ocean.

Colobus Conservation

Visit this sanctuary dedicated to protecting Kenya's endangered colobus monkeys.

Shimba Hills

Day trip to this national reserve for wildlife viewing and stunning waterfalls.

Local Cuisine

Enjoy fresh seafood and Swahili dishes at beachfront restaurants.

Nightlife

Beach bars and clubs offering live music and cocktails under the stars.
